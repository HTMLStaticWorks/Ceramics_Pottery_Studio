About Clay & Craft

Refining the Art of Ceramics

"We believe clay is the language of the soul."

Our mission is to create a sanctuary where the ancient craft of pottery meets modern artistic expression. We provide the tools, the knowledge, and the space for individuals to reconnect with their creative essence.

Every piece formed in our studio tells a story of patience, persistence, and the raw beauty of imperfection. We are more than a studio; we are a community of makers dedicated to the lifelong journey of mastery.

The Genesis

Founded on the Wheel

Establish in 1994, Clay&Craft began in a small basement with just two spinning wheels and a single kiln. Our founder, Sarah Vost, envisioned a workspace where artists didn't have to choose between professional equipment and a welcoming atmosphere.

Today, we've expanded across eight studios, but our philosophy remains unchanged: process over perfection, community over competition.
