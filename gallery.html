Submitting work for the wall

We rotate the gallery collection every six weeks to showcase the evolving talent of our community.

  • ✓ Submission Limit: Up to 3 pieces per member per rotation.
  • ✓ Requirements: Include a short artist statement (max 150 words).
  • ✓ Firing: Work must be fired at Clay & Kiln or an approved partner.
  • ✓ Deadline: Submit by the 15th of the month.

Email photos and dimensions to gallery@clayklin.studio

Filter tips

Finding the right inspiration is easy with our process-based filters. Here is how we categorize work in the studio:

Wheel thrown: Best for functional ware like cups, bowls, and vases.
Hand built: Focused on slabs, coils, and pinch-pot techniques.
Sculptural: Explore figurative or abstract artistic expressions.
Glazing: Deep dives into color, surface studies, and firing effects.
