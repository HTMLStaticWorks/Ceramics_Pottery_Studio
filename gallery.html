Submitting work for the wall

We rotate the gallery every six weeks. Members can submit up to three pieces per rotation with a short artist statement (150 words max). Work must be fired at ⌘ Clay & Kiln or approved partner studios. Email photos and dimensions to gallery@clayklin.studio by the 15th of the month before each hang.

Visiting the gallery

Public hours

Tuesday–Sunday · 11:00–19:00

No ticket required — enter through the main studio lobby and follow signs to the gallery nook.

Artist talks

Opening receptions on the first Sunday after each rotation (17:00–19:00). RSVP via the events list in your dashboard.

Filter tips

Use Wheel thrown for cups, bowls, and vases; Hand built for slabs and coils; Sculptural for figurative or abstract work; Glazing for close-ups of surface and color. Mixed pieces appear under “All.”
