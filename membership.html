Membership FAQs

Is there a joining fee?

We charge a one-time ₹1,200 orientation and handbook fee for your first month. It covers your first bag of reclaim clay.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes — one freeze per year up to 60 days for travel or medical reasons. Kiln credits pause with your billing.

Do tiers include class discounts?

Studio Member and Master Potter receive 15% off drop-in workshops. Clay Starter pays full price but can use class packs.
