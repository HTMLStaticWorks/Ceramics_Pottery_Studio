Common Inquiries

How do I start my membership? +

Simply select a plan and complete the registration. After verification, we'll schedule a mandatory 30-minute studio induction to walk you through our safety protocols and equipment usage.

Are materials included in the fee? +

Members have access to our house glazes. Clay and specific specialty materials can be purchased from our on-site shop at member-only rates.

Can I change my plan later? +

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership at the beginning of any billing cycle through your member portal.

Is there a contract? +

Our 24/7 Pro access requires a 3-month commitment. Basic and Premium plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts.

What tools should I bring? +

Basic tools are provided. However, most members prefer to build their own professional kit over time. Storage lockers are available for Premium and Pro members.
