How registration works

1 · Browse & save a seat

Pick a class that matches your level. Seat counts refresh every morning at 6:00. Logged-in members can hold one seat for 15 minutes while you check out.

2 · Pay & confirm

Complete payment or use a class credit from your membership. You will receive a calendar invite and a prep list by email.

3 · Show up prepared

Arrive 10 minutes early to apron up. Clay and basic tools are included; specialty tools are noted on each class page.

4 · Make-up policy

Cancel up to 48 hours before start for a full credit. Late cancellations forfeit the class fee unless we fill your seat from the waitlist.

What to bring & studio etiquette

Wear clothes you can move in — clay washes out, but some glazes stain. Closed-toe shoes are required on the wheel floor. Tie long hair back. We provide aprons, towels, and recycled clay; you bring curiosity and patience.

Please respect shared tables: clean your wheel and tools after each session, label all work with your name and date, and ask before moving someone else’s drying pieces. Phones on silent during demos so everyone can hear adjustments.

Class FAQs

Do I need a membership to take a class?

No — drop-in classes are open to everyone. Members receive priority booking during the first 48 hours of each release calendar.

Can I retake a foundations class?

Yes. Many members repeat Wheel Foundations to build muscle memory. Ask your instructor for a progression checklist when you are ready.

Where do my pieces go after class?

Work stays on the studio shelf until bisque. You will book kiln time through your dashboard when pieces are dry enough to fire.
